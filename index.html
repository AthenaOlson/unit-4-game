the crystal collector

  • A random number is slected at the start of each game.
  • Click on one of the four crystals to add a specific number of points to your total score.
  • Win the game by matching your total score to the randomly selected number.
  • Lose the game if your total score goes above the randomly selected number.
  • When the game restarts, the game will select a new value for each crystal.
